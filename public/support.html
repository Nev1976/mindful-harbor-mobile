Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new habit?
Go to the Habits tab and tap the "+" button to add a new mindfulness habit. Choose a name, description, and color for easy tracking.
Can I customize my daily reflection prompts?
Our carefully curated prompts are designed by mindfulness experts. Premium users will have access to custom prompt categories in future updates.
How do streaks work?
Complete your habits daily to build streaks. Missing a day resets your streak, but you can always start fresh the next day.
Is my data private?
Yes, your reflections and personal data are completely private and stored securely. We never share your personal information.
How do I reset my progress?
Contact support if you need to reset your progress. We recommend continuing your journey as each day builds toward lasting mindfulness.
